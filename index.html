Our Story

Born from the
heart of India

Spice & Soul Café was born from a simple belief: that India's rich culinary heritage deserves a modern, soulful café stage — where a masala cortado sits beside a flaky croissant without apology.

We source our arabica beans directly from the Coorg and Chikmagalur hills, and our spices from Kerala's finest farms — ensuring every cup carries the story of the soil.

Our bakers arrive before dawn to craft breads and pastries with Indian flavours woven in — cardamom, saffron, jeera, and rose water.
